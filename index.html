Research

My research interest lies at the intersection of programming languages and databases. I am maintaining the BIRDS project that supports using the Datalog language to program bidirectional transformations in databases.

Education

  • 2017 - 2022: Ph.D. degree from Department of Informatics, School of Multidisciplinary Sciences, The Graduate University for Advanced Studies, SOKENDAI, Japan (in alliance with National Institute of Informatics)

  • 2012 - 2017: B.Sc. degree from School of Information and Communication Technology, Hanoi University of Science and Technology.

Experience

  • 2023 - present: Lecturer at Department of Computer Science, School of Information and Communication Technology (SoICT), Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST), Vietnam.
