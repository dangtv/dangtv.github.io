Thanks for visiting my web site :)) My name is Dang Tran (Trần Văn Đặng). I am an undergraduate student at Center for Training of Excellent Students, School of Information and Communication Technology, Hanoi University of Science and Technology (Vietnam). Currently, I live in Hanoi, Vietnam, where I study in my university and work as a research assistant about computer science in my laboratory.

When I was a freshman at university, I was most interested in Mathematics, so I spent a lot of time learning my favorite subject. Then, with many efforts, I won the First Prize in 21st National Mathematics Olympiad for Students in 2013. Since 2015, I have been focusing on research projects at my laboratory and the mathematical background has really been helping me solve a lot of problems in my work. My research interests include cloud computing, distributed system and machine learning. My current work involves designing and implementing novel efficient power management policies based on queueing models for server farms as well as proactive auto-scaling techniques in cloud environments. I also believe that the large data generated by the operation of cloud system has a lot of opportunities and challenges to analysis and develop more efficient models.
